About & Approach

From Minds to Machines

My career began with one question: How does the brain decide what is real? That curiosity led from undergraduate cog-sci labs through a Psychology PhD on perception errors and a Psychiatry postdoc on cognitive remediation. When LLMs started hallucinating and strategising much like the patients I'd studied, the overlap was impossible to ignore.

Today I convert insights from 35 papers and 27k participants into alignment frameworks for advanced AI.
