The Science: Neural and Computational Mechanisms

Neural Network Findings

Brain Network Analysis: Default (orange) and Frontoparietal (green) Networks

My research revealed that individuals prone to apophenia show increased default network coherence but decreased frontoparietal control network coherence.

These findings illuminate the openness-psychoticism dimension and its neural correlates, showing how individual differences in pattern detection stem from fundamental differences in brain network organization.
