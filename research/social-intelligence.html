Human Social Cognition

Social cognitive abilities include processes that allow us to understand the minds of others‚Äîincluding emotion perception and theory of mind. My research has shown that social cognitive abilities exhibit a dual-use nature. Many of the capabilities underpinning compassion also enable manipulative deception. As AI systems develop increasingly complex social intelligence, we must ensure these abilities are channeled for prosocial means.

From Human Theory of Mind to AI Alignment

Just as humans with enhanced theory of mind can choose cooperation or manipulation, AI systems with ToM capabilities face the same crossroads. This is not a theoretical concern‚Äîit's an existential challenge.

An AI that can model human beliefs at multiple levels could predict and manipulate human behavior with unprecedented sophistication. My research on human social cognition provides crucial insights for navigating this challenge.
