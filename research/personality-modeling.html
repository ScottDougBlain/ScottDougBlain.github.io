Personality as a Control System

A major pillar of my research has been connecting personality trait extremes to cognitive vulnerabilities and psychopathology.

Empirical Anchors

  • Openness ‚Üí Apophenia/Psychosis Risk
  • Low Agreeableness ‚Üí Manipulation, Callousness, Deceit
  • Low Extraversion + High Neuroticism ‚Üí Depression Risk
  • Low Extraversion + Low Openness ‚Üí Low Behavioral Exploration/Autism
