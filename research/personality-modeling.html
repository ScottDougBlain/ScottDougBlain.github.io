Research Focus: Trait Extremes & Dysfunction

Openness/Intellect Imbalance

High Risk
Very High Openness + Low Intellect ‚Üí Apophenia

My research shows how overactive pattern detection without reality-testing leads to false positives and hallucination-like experiences.

Agreeableness Dysfunction

High Risk
Very Low Agreeableness ‚Üí Antagonism

I've identified how impaired cooperation systems manifest as manipulation, callousness, and strategic deception.

Reward System Collapse

High Risk
Very Low Extraversion + High Neuroticism ‚Üí Anhedonia

My work demonstrates how combined reward insensitivity and threat hypersensitivity create depressive spirals.
